Circles Help


Adding persons to a circle

a. Press the people picker button and select persons or groups to insert.

b. Drag and drop person cards or group cards from the "Contacts" app.

c. Go to the File-menu and click "Add Cards...".


Finding persons in a circle

Go to the Edit-menu, open the find-submenu and click "Find Contact..."


Adding entries to a person card

a. Write in the textview and hit enter.

b. Click the edit-button of an entry in the list of entries to edit in the textview and re-enter.

c. Copy (cmd-c) an entry and paste in the textview to edit and re-enter.


Changing the date of an entry

Change the date of an entry by pressing the date button and specifying the accurate date for your entry.


Tags

You can give keywords or tags to your entries by using the "#"sign before your tag. Tags can be used to group entries and facilitate searching.


Points

a. Points can be added to an entry by entering "@a(-- here is where the points go --)". 

Example: "Today the student accomplished 3 project milestones @a(3)."

b. You can also click the "Add Points (plus)" button to add points.


Ratings

a. Ratings can be added to an entry by entering "@p( --here is where the achieved score goes--/--here is where the score range goes--)".

Example: "The first question was answered completely @p(10/10), and the second was complete to the most part @p(5/6). Excellent."

b. You can also click the "Add Ratings (star)" button to add ratings.


Exporting a report

You can export a report in the *.csv file format for a person card by selecting the Menu-Item "File">"Export List of Entries">"As CSV".

Import the exported file as text-file in the spreadsheet software of your choice. Specify "|" as separator.
